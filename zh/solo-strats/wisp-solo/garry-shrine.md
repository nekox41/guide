# Garry Shrine

## **At the Shrine – VS Timing**

At the same instant the loot lure pops:

* Go Operator
* Use the [**Power Transfer**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Focus/Madurai#Power_Transfer) duration as a reference to time your **VS**

## **Timing Pre-VS**

You have to cast **VS** **7 seconds** after the loot lure explodes

If you are constant enough then:

{% columns %}
{% column width="75%" %}
\* Wait until **13s left** on Power Transfer

* Activate **Void Strike**, switch back to your frame
* Insert the **Shard**
* Teleport to CL using Breach Surge
* What follows depends on the Garry spawn
{% endcolumn %}

## Garry Fight

* Use [<mark style="color:$success;">**Mandonel**</mark>](../../advanced/mandonel-voltless.md) to prevent Raplak Yellow Crits
* Use **Eclipse DR** (Night mode) if needed for survivability
  * Double tapping **Wil-O-Wisp** makes you invulnerable for 3s

## After the Down

* Use the undervomvs to charge your lures
* Fetch 2 more lures
* Perform the Garry capshot (Eclipse Day mode)
* Return to the shrine
