# Loc-Pins

[**Loc-pins**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Loc-Pin) will help you position yourself correctly; they’re a great time-saver and QOL improvement.

* Loc-Pins can be researched in a Clan Dojo’s Tenno Research Lab
