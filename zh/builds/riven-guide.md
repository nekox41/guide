# Riven Guide

## Primary - Zenith

**Dual-Stats**

1. [**MS CD negative**](https://warframe.market/auctions/search?type=riven\&positive_stats=critical_damage,multishot\&negative_stats=has\&sort_by=price_asc\&weapon_url_name=zenith) ⇨ 3 Multishot Solo/Duo Build\
   Probably the best option in terms of flexibility
2. [**CC CD negative**](https://warframe.market/auctions/search?type=riven\&positive_stats=critical_chance,critical_damage\&negative_stats=has\&sort_by=price_asc\&weapon_url_name=zenith) ⇨ 2 Multishot Solo Build ⇨ Replace Critical Delay\
   This Riven works, no specific grade, and it's really cheap
3. [**MS DMG negative**](https://warframe.market/auctions/search?type=riven\&positive_stats=multishot,base_damage_%2F_melee_damage\&negative_stats=has\&sort_by=price_asc\&weapon_url_name=zenith) ⇨ 3 Multishot Solo/Duo Build\
   Lower Than MS CD

**Tri-Stats**

1. [**MS CD FR negative**](https://warframe.market/auctions/search?type=riven\&positive_stats=multishot,fire_rate_%2F_attack_speed,critical_damage\&negative_stats=has\&sort_by=price_asc\&weapon_url_name=zenith) ⇨ 2 Multishot Duo Build ⇨ Replace Fire Rate
2. [**MS DMG FR negative**](https://warframe.market/auctions/search?type=riven\&positive_stats=multishot,base_damage_%2F_melee_damage,fire_rate_%2F_attack_speed\&negative_stats=has\&sort_by=price_asc\&weapon_url_name=zenith) ⇨ 2 Multishot Duo Build ⇨ Replace Fire Rate

{% hint style="warning" %}
At least 100% Multishot is mandatory.\
These Rivens are often overkill and very expensive, but they allow you to run 10 fire rate with 2ms
{% endhint %}

## Melee - Vastilok

{% hint style="warning" %}
Only get a riven if you plan to use **Secondary Outburst .** Vastilok rivens tend to be hard to find and thus expensive
{% endhint %}

**Dual-Stats**

1. **AS CCC negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
   Combo Count Chance allows you to quickly increase your combo and synergizes with [**Outburst**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Secondary_Outburst)\
   Attack speed isn’t necessarily a required stat, since your build already has plenty of it, still ok
2. **IC CCC negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
   The higher your initial combo, the faster you will reach x12
3. **IC AS negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
   The higher your initial combo, the faster you will reach x12

**Tri-Stats**

* **AS CCC IC negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
  Clearly overkill and overpriced, but if you have it, play it.
