{% hint style="info" %}
* [**Arcane Tanker**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Arcane_Tanker) synergizes with [**Primary Bulwark**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Primary_Bulwark) for Zenith
  * This means you **don't** need to run Violet Archon Shards for [**Electric Damage**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Violet_Archon_Shard)**.**
    * If you prefer, you can take Tanker off and use Violet Archon Shards instead
  * Tanker lasts 1 minute but it can't be refreshed, use your Archgun at least 30s after the last limb of each Eidolon
* **Low duration** is perfectly ok
  * [**Wormhole**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Wormhole) has no duration and [**Roar**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Roar) is only used for Waters (buff yourself and your host at the shrine)
* **Sprint Boost** aura can be swapped, but your Host is more than likely running **Corrosive Projection** and you only want **one** of those to prevent too much **damage attenuation** from Propa
* [**Rolling Guard**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Rolling_Guard) is a flex slot, still recommended to stay alive
{% endhint %}
