# Amp & Operator Arcanes

## AMP Arcanes

1. [**Eternal Eradicate**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Eternal_Eradicate) On Operator Ability: +60% Damage to Amps for 8s
2. [**Eternal Onslaught**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Eternal_Onslaught) +180% Critical Chance for 8 seconds after depleting Operator energy

## Operator Arcanes

1. [**Magus Repair**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Repair) or [**Magus Elevate**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Elevate) to heal your Warframe
2. [**Magus Melt**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Melt) up to 210% Heat Damage to your Amp, it aids in breaking Limbs with your Amp
