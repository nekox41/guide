# How to Break Shield + Limb

## Conditions

* Have Raplak ([<mark style="color:$success;">**127/ 177/ 123**</mark>](../builds/amp-builds-modules.md))
* [<mark style="color:$success;">**Mod**</mark>](../builds/amp-mods-thara.md) your AMP correctly
* [**Magus Melt**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Melt)
* [**Volt Shields**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Electric_Shield)
  * If you play Voltless, you will need the [**Mandonel**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel) archgun

## Gameplay

* Before shooting the Limb, place a [**Volt Shield**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Electric_Shield) between you and Eidolon
  * Stack 3x Volt Shields for the Harry for the low chance of a yellow crit
  * Voltless check → [<mark style="color:$success;">**Mandonel**</mark>](../advanced/mandonel-voltless.md)
* You must stack Magus Melt x7 → [<mark style="color:$success;">**Void Sling faster**</mark>](how-to-void-sling-faster.md)
* Proc your [**Eternal Onslaught**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Eternal_Onslaught) and [**Eradicate Arcanes**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Eternal_Eradicate)
* Once the Limb is vulnerable, shoot it
  * For faster limbs learn to [<mark style="color:$success;">**predict Raplak**</mark>](raplak-and-propa-timing.md)

Congratulations! You took the Shield and Limb in one shot
