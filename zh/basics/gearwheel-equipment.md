# Gearwheel Equipment

<details>

<summary><strong>Archwing Launcher</strong></summary>

* Requires [**Archwing Launcher**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archwing_Launcher)
* [**Itzal**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Itzal) **is highly recommended** (obtained from Clan Dojo)
  * Cosmic Crush (3): Pulls vomvs and loot

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Ciphers</strong></summary>

* [**100x cipher BP**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Cipher) from Clan Dojo
  * _36k Ferrite & 36k Nano Spores_
* Allows for instant lure hacking (Y)

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Squad Energy Restore (energy pads)</strong></summary>

* [**100x energy pad BP**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Squad_Energy_Restore) from Clan Dojo
  * _5k Polymer Bundle & 30k Nano Spores_
* Pulses 100 Energy every 7.5s for 30s
* Great for emergency situations

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Air Support Charges: Med-Tower</strong></summary>

* [**100x air support BP**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Air_Support_Charges) from Clan Dojo
  * _12k Ferrite, 20k Salvage, 10 Morphics & 7k Plastids_
* It has collisions, so you can **detonate Propa** against it
* Greatly useful for **Nova stats**

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Loc-Pin</strong></summary>

* [**BP**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Loc-Pin) can be obtained from Clan Dojo
* Permanent location marks

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>On-Call-Crew</strong></summary>

* There's a more detailed segment for this: [<mark style="color:$success;">**OCC**</mark>](https://fenomeral.gitbook.io/eidolon-guide/basics/on-call-crew-occ)

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Archgun </strong><mark style="color:$info;"><strong>(optional)</strong></mark></summary>

* Any: Can equip [**Primary**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Primary_Dexterity) and [**Secondary Dexterity**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Secondary_Dexterity) for the passive **melee combo duration**
  * Requires [**Archgun Arcane Adapter**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archgun_Arcane_Adapter)
* [**Mandonel**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel) only: Buff Amp’s **Damage** and **Critical Chance**
  * Requires [**Archgun Deployer**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archgun_Deployer)

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Necramech </strong><mark style="color:$info;"><strong>(optional)</strong></mark></summary>

* Useful to keep Cetus Gate open between runs
* Can shoot undervomvs

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>K-drive </strong><mark style="color:$info;"><strong>(optional)</strong></mark></summary>

* Can keep Cetus door open
* **Can fix many bugs,** basically, if you encounter a bug always try calling your K-drive and see if that fixes it (most times it will)
* Makes you immune to magnetic procs from the Eidolon (rarely if ever used for this)
* [**Assembled K-Drives**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/K-Drive#Crafting) can equip the [**Juice mod**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Juice) to regenerate energy

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Ancient Healer </strong><mark style="color:$info;"><strong>(highly optional)</strong></mark></summary>

* Obtained from New Loka Syndicate
* Status immunity aura, includes knockdowns

</details>

{% hint style="info" %}
Don't forget to equip gear items in your [**Gearwheel**](https://warframe.fandom.com/wiki/Equipment#Gear) \
Then add key binds for them under [**Settings > Controls**](https://warframe.fandom.com/wiki/Key_Bindings)
