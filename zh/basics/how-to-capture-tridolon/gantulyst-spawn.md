# Gantulyst Spawn

To spawn the Gantulyst , you must collect a [**Brilliant Eidolon Shard**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Brilliant_Eidolon_Shard), which is awarded after successfully capturing a Teralyst.\
Once obtained, bring the shard to the Shrine (Gara Toht Lake) and deposit it to summon the Gantulyst.
