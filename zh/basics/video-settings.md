# Video Settings

#### **Texture Memory&#x20;**<mark style="color:blue;">**HIGH**</mark>

<figure><img src="https://2250278911-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FSwotjVkQd1faB6N1f6j4%2Fuploads%2Fuien1rohiGPguAASJF7y%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d7bfd1d0-6a91-4208-a4da-01478e56cf12" alt=""><figcaption><p>Make sure to keep this on <strong>High</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### **Volumetric Lighting&#x20;**<mark style="color:$danger;">**OFF**</mark>**&#x20;& Volumetric Fog Quality&#x20;**<mark style="color:$danger;">**DISABLED**</mark>

<figure><img src="https://2250278911-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FSwotjVkQd1faB6N1f6j4%2Fuploads%2Fy5TVB7vfsD4oHWK6BSOS%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=75cd24a0-0fa9-4ad2-bcff-fd7b789d315c" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Removes fog/mist</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### **Particle Quality LOW & GPU Particle Quality&#x20;**<mark style="color:$danger;">**DISABLED**</mark>

<figure><img src="https://2250278911-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FSwotjVkQd1faB6N1f6j4%2Fuploads%2FLy1D832BDIPK2lTNlSkM%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=b8179756-36d7-446a-9720-27140c334907" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Particles are pretty distracting, disables the confetti</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### **Distortions&#x20;**<mark style="color:$danger;">**OFF**</mark>

<figure><img src="https://2250278911-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FSwotjVkQd1faB6N1f6j4%2Fuploads%2FBXCXQtSPNJylg1PvK9vI%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e1aa27a5-6496-4511-90af-da7118819459" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Makes it easier to spot the Teralyst at a distance</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### **Color Correction&#x20;**<mark style="color:$danger;">**OFF**</mark>

<figure><img src="https://2250278911-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FSwotjVkQd1faB6N1f6j4%2Fuploads%2FDKAu1Rq1YE1AenZW0TCj%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=9bd407b4-97c5-4350-abe1-c43b20e088d7" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>This one is quite important to prevents flashbangs</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
For **Speedrunners** trying for the leaderboard we recommend **turning off the rest of the settings** to keep your frame rate as stable as possible.

Alternatively upscaling with **DLSS** on **Performance** or **Ultra Performance** is decent enough and allows your game to stay "pretty"
{% endhint %}
