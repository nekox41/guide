## Long Spawn

The long spawn is a special case, the Eidolon appears underground, causing an additional delay when breaking its shield and limbs.\
This delay can be **bypassed** by using your Amp's alt fire, either [**Propa**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Propa_Scaffold) **or** [**Shraksun**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Shraksun_Scaffold)**.**\
We use the [**Zenith**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Zenith), whose passive allows us to destroy the limbs.

<figure><img src="https://1960884447-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMnWMWMKLrFw5Len33GHR%2Fuploads%2F9Crt8ARDMkvdewdtd4ve%2Funknown.png?alt=media&#x26;token=7198b895-3504-4494-93bd-170fdb6daf7b" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
**Zenith** can only be acquired from the **Daily Tribute** system (or additional copies from Cephalon Simaris), so it will take you up to 100 days to get it; if you don’t have it, [**Furis Incarnon**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Furis_Incarnon_Genesis) can be a good alternative, though less effective.
{% endhint %}
