# QOL Settings

* **Fire Manual Trigger Weapons Continuosly** ON
* **Enemy Highlights** ON
  * **Enemy Highlight Color/Intensity** to your liking
* **Double Jump executes Void Sling** ON
* **Customize Key bindings > Jump** to Mouse Wheel Up or Down
