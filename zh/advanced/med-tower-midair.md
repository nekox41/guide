# Med-Tower Midair

To get the [**Med-Tower**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mantis#Med-Tower), you just need to obtain the [**Mantis**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mantis) landing craft.

Then configure your [**Air Support**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Air_Support_Charges): press Escape ➛ Orbiter ➛ Landing Craft ➛ **Air Support** ➛ Select Med-Tower

{% hint style="info" %}
Med-Tower usually spawns on the ground, however you can place it suspended in midair by using certain Warframe abilities or projectiles that will act as a temporary "ground" for the Med-Tower
{% endhint %}

## Volt

1. Carry a Volt Shield with you
2. Melee out of your Archwing (looking up to avoid ground slamming)
3. [**Aim Glide**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Maneuvers#Aim_Glide)
4. Press your Med-Tower key bind ([<mark style="color:$success;">**Gear Hotkey key bindings**</mark>](../basics/gearwheel-equipment.md))

## Nova

1. Melee out of your Archwing (looking up to avoid ground slamming)
2. [**Aim Glide**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Maneuvers#Aim_Glide) looking down
3. Cast [**Antimatter Drop**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Antimatter_Drop) (2 by default)
4. Press your Med-Tower key bind ([<mark style="color:$success;">**Gear Hotkey key bindings**</mark>](../basics/gearwheel-equipment.md))

## Wisp <a href="#how-to-place-med-tower-for-wisp" id="how-to-place-med-tower-for-wisp"></a>

1. Melee out of your Archwing (looking up to avoid ground slamming)
2. [**Aim Glide**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Maneuvers#Aim_Glide) looking down
3. Cast [**Will-O-Wisp**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Wil-O-Wisp) (2 by default)
4. Press your Med-Tower key bind ([<mark style="color:$success;">**Gear Hotkey key bindings**</mark>](../basics/gearwheel-equipment.md))

<figure><img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/SwotjVkQd1faB6N1f6j4/blobs/DlVWOL7TacXZW5B30M1O/image" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Mandonel (any Warframe)

1. Fire [**Mandonel**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel) **charged shot** just below of where you want your Med-Tower, this can be from a Necramech or your Archgun Deployer
2. Now on your Archwing, fly over Mandonel's trail
3. Quickly melee out and then press your Med-Tower key bind ([<mark style="color:$success;">**Gear Hotkey key bindings**</mark>](../basics/gearwheel-equipment.md))
