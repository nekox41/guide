# Mandonel Voltless

## Why Mandonel?

**Raplak** can only reach <mark style="color:orange;">**197.2% CC**</mark>, so even tought you will <mark style="color:orange;">**Orange Crit**</mark> most of the time you still will <mark style="color:yellow;">**Yellow Crit 2.8%**</mark> of the time

You'd want to use **Mandonel** for consistency, a <mark style="color:yellow;">**Yellow Crit**</mark> won't one tap Eidolon Limbs

For Harry even an Orange Crit won't one tap without **Mandonel's** damage boosting

**Mandonel –** [**Charged Shot effects**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel)**:**

* **x2 Damage**
* **+10% flat Critical Chance**
* **+50% Critical Damage**
* Trail duration: \~8 seconds
  * We recommend firing near the end of the Eidolon's cry to acomodate for the low duration
* Usage: From the gear wheel through the [**Archgun Deployer**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archgun_Deployer)
  * Mandonel from a <mark style="color:$danger;">Necramech won't work</mark>

The trails are wide and easy to spot, but make sure to use bright colors if necessary.

## Mandonel Recoil Skip:

You can fire Mandonel charged shot by using transference (5), thus avoiding the recoil

Inputs:

1. Fully charge Mandonel and **hold it** until ready to fire (hold left mouse button)
2. To release, switch to Operator mode (5 by default)
3. The shot will be fired instantly without any recoil
